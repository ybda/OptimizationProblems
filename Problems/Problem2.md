* 200 resistors
* 120 transistors
* 150 capacitors

and is required to produce two types of circuits.

**Type A**

* 20 resistors
* 10 transistors
* 10 capacitors

Profit: £5

**Type B**
Requires:
* 10 resistors
* 20 transistors
* 30 capacitors

Profit: £12

How many of each circuit should be produced to maximize profit?
